# M004 Context — Product-Level Intelligence + Grounded AI Layer

## Why this moved to M004

This milestone remains strategically important, but it should not execute before M003. The grouped-intelligence and grounded-AI layer will only be credible if the acquisition system feeding it is stronger, broader, and better bounded than the current steady-state FR page-1 loop.

## Milestone intent

Turn the radar from a strong listing-level and segment-level reader into a grounded product-intelligence layer that can reason about recurring product/group entities, surface evidence-backed inline insights, generate periodic syntheses, and answer analytical questions without pretending to be an oracle.

## Core requirements advanced here

- R013 — cautious product/article-level grouping
- R014 — grounded inline insights and periodic syntheses
- R015 — grounded analytical copilote

## Guardrails

- False merges are worse than false splits.
- AI outputs must remain grounded first in collected evidence and grouped entities.
- Observation, inference, AI interpretation, and broader hypothesis must stay visibly separated.
- Weekly synthesis is the primary narrative cadence; shorter briefs must be eventful, not filler.

## Outcome to preserve

When this milestone is complete, grouped market views, inline AI interpretation, weekly syntheses, and the copilote should all operate on real radar data with explicit evidence windows, confidence, abstention behavior, and provenance back to observed listings.
