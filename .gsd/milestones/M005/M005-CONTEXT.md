# M005 Context — SaaS Hardening and Commercialization

## Why this moved to M005

Commercialization still matters, but it should follow both the acquisition-scale work in M003 and the grouped-intelligence layer in M004. The goal is to harden and package a system that is already analytically strong, not to commercialize an underpowered collector or a shallow AI dashboard.

## Milestone intent

Evolve the radar into a durable SaaS product that is operationally supportable, commercially presentable, and still clearly evidence-first.

## Themes expected here

- hosted/runtime hardening
- auth/account/tenant boundaries
- support and admin operations
- commercial product flows, saved workspaces, and packaging
- launch-readiness proof that checks reliability and product quality together

## Guardrails

- Commercial polish must not weaken evidence, confidence, or uncertainty surfaces.
- Hosted operation must stay observable and supportable.
- Credibility remains the anchor even as the surface becomes more productized.

## Outcome to preserve

When this milestone is complete, the product should feel launchable and supportable without losing the thing that makes it worth launching: trustworthy evidence-backed market intelligence.
