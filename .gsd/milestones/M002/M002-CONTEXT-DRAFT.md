# M002: Enriched Market Intelligence Experience — Context Draft

**Gathered:** 2026-03-17
**Status:** Draft — needs dedicated discussion before planning

## Seed From Current Discussion

M002 is the milestone that should deepen the market reading after M001 has proven the listing-level radar loop in real local conditions. It should not replace the evidence-first foundation built in M001. It should enrich it.

The user wants M002 to make the product more developed, richer, and more complete. That includes stronger market reading, richer user-facing utility, and better product experience — not just more calculations hidden behind the scenes.

## What This Milestone Likely Advances

- stronger contextual reading of the market by sub-category, brand, condition, and price band where support is sufficient
- richer ways to inspect and compare segments once the M001 evidence base is trustworthy
- more complete user-facing utility features such as comparisons, exports, and other helpful workflows
- a more visually polished and comfortable dashboard experience without losing analytical sobriety or evidence linkage
- continued preservation of observed / inferred / uncertain separation as the product becomes richer

## Candidate User-Visible Outcome

When this milestone is complete, the user should be able to move beyond the first credible radar read and use the product as a richer market intelligence workspace:

- compare segments more intentionally
- inspect broader market movement with stronger contextual baselines
- use richer utility features that make analysis easier to reuse and act on
- enjoy a more complete, more polished product surface without sacrificing trust

## Candidate Capabilities

- deeper contextual scoring and market normalization beyond the lightweight M001 approach
- stronger trend views and segment-level interpretation
- comparisons between sub-categories or market slices
- export capabilities for selected views or results
- richer dashboard UX and interaction patterns
- additional product features that improve usability even when they are not directly core to the ranking model

## Constraints To Preserve

- M002 must not turn the product into a visually rich but analytically weak dashboard
- any new summaries or comparisons must remain backed by listing-level evidence
- the product must keep showing coverage, confidence, freshness, and uncertainty in ways the user can actually see
- richer utility is welcome, but not at the cost of the radar’s credibility

## Why This Is Drafted Instead Of Finalized Now

M002 depends heavily on what M001 proves in practice:

- how broad the real coverage becomes
- how strong the revisit history is
- where the first scoring model is already sufficient versus weak
- which product utilities will matter most once the M001 dashboard exists in code

A dedicated discussion should happen after M001 has changed the codebase and revealed real tradeoffs.

## Open Questions For Future Discussion

- Which enriched product features are truly core for M002 versus nice-to-have?
- How far should M002 push contextual analytics before M003 takes over with product-level grouping and AI?
- Which comparison and export workflows would create the most real value first?
- How much design polish belongs in M002 versus later SaaS hardening?
