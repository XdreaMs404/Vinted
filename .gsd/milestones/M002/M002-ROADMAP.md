# M002: Enriched Market Intelligence Experience

## Vision
Keep the French-first M002 product surfaces delivered in S01-S09, but replace the underlying monolithic SQLite boundary with a production-grade polyglot data platform that can retain raw evidence cheaply, serve large-corpus analytics at low latency, keep mutable runtime truth bounded, and prepare grounded AI without another future storage rearchitecture.
