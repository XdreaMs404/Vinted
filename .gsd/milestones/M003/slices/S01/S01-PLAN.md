# S01: Conservative product grouping with confidence + provenance

**Goal:** Introduce the first real product-group entity surface by shipping cautious grouping, explicit confidence bands, provenance, and member-conflict visibility through a usable grouped-products UI on top of the existing listing truth.
**Demo:** After this: A user opens a real grouped-products surface in the dashboard/explorer and can inspect strong product merges, probable groups, and deliberate non-merges on live radar data, with provenance explaining why listings were grouped and links back to member listings.
